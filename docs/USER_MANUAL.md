# TenantFlow — Tenant (User) Manual

A simple guide for **tenants** — the people renting a home. TenantFlow lets you see your
rent, pay bills, raise complaints, read notices, and keep your documents in one place.

> **Demo login:** `tenant@tenantflow.app` / `TenantFlow@2026`

---

## 1. Getting started

### Your login
Your landlord/property manager creates your account and gives you an **email** and a
**temporary password**. You don't sign up yourself.

### First sign-in
1. Open the app → **Sign in**.
2. Enter the email (or phone) and password your landlord gave you.
3. You land on your **Dashboard**.

### Change your password (recommended)
1. Go to **Settings** (in the "More" section of the menu).
2. Under **Change password**, enter your current password and a new one → **Update password**.

---

## 2. Dashboard

Your home screen summarizes everything important:

- A **welcome card** with your property, room number, and move-in details.
- **Quick actions** to jump to paying rent or raising a complaint.
- **Summary cards**: monthly rent, latest electricity/water bills, outstanding dues, deposit.
- **Upcoming payments**, **recent notices**, and **recent payments** at a glance.

---

## 3. Rent

Menu → **Rent**.

- See your **monthly rent**, **next due date**, deposit, and any **outstanding balance**
  (including penalties if you're late).
- Click **Pay rent** → choose a payment method (UPI / Card / Net Banking / Cash) → confirm.
- Your **payment history** is listed below, and you can **download a receipt** for each paid
  month.

---

## 4. Bills (utilities)

Menu → **Bills**.

- See your **electricity** and **water** bills, what's unpaid, and what's overdue.
- Click **Pay** to settle unpaid utility bills. Paid bills show a receipt.

---

## 5. Payments

Menu (More) → **Payments** — a complete history of every payment you've made (rent,
utilities, penalties) with the amount, method, date, and **receipt number**, plus your
total paid.

---

## 6. Complaints

Menu → **Complaints** — raise and track maintenance issues.

1. Click **Raise complaint**.
2. Enter a title, choose a category (plumbing, electrical, etc.) and priority, say where the
   issue is, and describe it → **Submit**.
3. You get a **reference number**. Track the status as your landlord works on it:
   **Open → In progress → Resolved**.
4. The **Active Requests** section shows what's still being worked on; the history table
   shows everything.

---

## 7. Notices

Menu (More) → **Notices** — announcements from your property manager.

- Unread notices have a **blue dot**. Click a notice to expand its full details; opening it
  marks it as read.

---

## 8. Documents

Menu (More) → **Documents** — files your landlord shares with you (e.g. your rental
agreement, receipts).

- Click **Download** on any document to save it to your device.

---

## 9. Settings

Menu (More) → **Settings**:

- **Profile** — update your occupation and emergency contact. (Your name, email, and phone
  are set by your landlord.)
- **Change password** — set a new password.

You can also switch between **light and dark mode** using the toggle in the top bar.

---

## 10. FAQ

**I didn't get a login.** Ask your property manager — they create tenant accounts.

**I forgot my password.** Ask your landlord to help; then change it in Settings once you're
in.

**Do I actually get charged when I pay?** In this version, payments are **recorded** and a
receipt is generated (no live card charge). Your landlord can turn on real online payments
later.

**Why is a bill marked "Overdue"?** It's past its due date and unpaid. Pay it from the Rent
or Bills page to clear it.

**Can I see another tenant's info?** No — you only ever see your own data.
